What style should the 3D structural element have?

All three rotate slowly and react to mouse hover. Pick the aesthetic that fits your portfolio best.

WIREFRAME

Wireframe Structure

A hollow wireframe building or truss — edges only, no fill. Matches blueprint aesthetic perfectly. Subtle green scan line sweeps through it. Minimal, technical, and fast to render.

Best performance Blueprint feel
SOLID

Solid Low-Poly Building

A low-polygon 3D building with flat shading — visible faces with subtle lighting. Looks modern and architectural. Rotates gently. More visually substantial than wireframe.

Modern look Slightly heavier
TRUSS

3D Structural Truss / Frame

A Pratt truss or space frame — the actual structure civil engineers design. Glowing joint nodes, rotating on all axes. Most unique and on-brand for a structural engineer portfolio.

Most on-brand Unique Custom geometry needed
